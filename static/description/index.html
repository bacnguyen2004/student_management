Student Management

Odoo 18 — Students, Courses, Enrollments

Features

  • 5 ORM models with business constraints and computed fields
  • Multi-view UI: List, Form, Kanban, Calendar, Graph, Pivot
  • Role-based security: Teacher, Staff, Administrator
  • Bulk enrollment wizard and PDF reports
  • Mail notifications and scheduled cron jobs
  • Unit tests with TransactionCase
